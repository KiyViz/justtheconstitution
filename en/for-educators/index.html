For Educators

justtheconstitution.org is built for the classroom. Ad-free, distraction-free, and available on any device your students use. No accounts, no logins, no cost.

How to use this in your classroom

The reading page is designed for both guided and independent study:

  • Assign specific sections using the direct links below — students land exactly where you want them.
  • The built-in citation engine generates Bluebook, MLA, and Chicago references automatically. Students copy a paragraph and get a properly formatted citation.
  • Reading progress is saved locally in each student's browser. They can resume where they left off without creating an account.
  • Works on phones, tablets, and laptops. Dark mode and adjustable text size accommodate different reading environments.

How students should cite

Every paragraph on the reading page has a copy button. Tap it to choose a citation format: Bluebook, MLA, Chicago, Markdown, or BibTeX.

The citation engine identifies the exact article, section, and clause automatically. No manual formatting required.
